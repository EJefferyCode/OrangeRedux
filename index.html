Our Agents







Jen Sandberg


In 1997, Jen graduated from Ohio State University with a Bachelors of Arts.
Having always had a passion for stories as a child, Jen dedicated her life
to finding the perfect story as well as publishing to books of her own. She
currently resides in New York City, has a dog and two daughters. Jen is one of the founders of The Orange Literary Agency.

John Beardman


John started his publishing career in 2010 after spending his early 20's
modeling in France for top designers such as Calvin Klein and Fendi Men.
In 2009, John finished his degree in English at NYU, all while developing
a soft spot for controversial romance, sci-fi and short stories. John currently resides in Hoboken, N.J. and loves running on the weekends.

Follow John On Twitter!

Danielle Len


Danielle Len was born and raised in Boston to two loving parents, Karen and Kevin.
After high school in 2001, she traveled(backpacked) through Europe and Africa,
developing a strong affliction for folklore tales as well as indiginous cuisine.
Today, Danielle's interests include coming home to her husband, a writer for
The New York Times, and going over the many great ideas and manuscripts she receives
on a daily basis!
